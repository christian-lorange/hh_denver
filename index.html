HHAp Denver is a free app for finding a great place for a drink or food in Denver Colorado. You can find locations based upon food and drink deals, locations with great outdoor spaces, and pet friendly establishments/patios.

Using the App - Different colors indicate current happy hour status. Yellow indicates happy hour will be starting within the next hour. Green are current happy hours. Red indicates happy hour is currently going on but will end in less than an hour. Times listed in the table indicate todays happy hour times.

In order to keep information accurate and out of date we pull current happy hour information from our server. This means you won't need to update your app to have the latest deals at your finger tips. If you aren't seeing happy hour times or deals please try refreshing your app.
